## Context
The default shape for course material: explain each topic at length, show a
worked solution, then offer practice.

## Decision
Rejected. Prose is capped at ≤2 sentences per concept (why/gotcha only) and
zero solutions may exist anywhere in shipped files — not in bodies, comments,
or "solution" sections. The learner attempts first, looks things up after.

## Alternatives considered
- Accepted alternative: assert-driven exercises — see DEC-0001.
- Collapsible/hidden solutions — rejected: still one click from defeating
  the method, and pollutes the repo with answers the lint gate must chase.

## Consequences
Recording this keeps future sessions from "helpfully" adding explanations,
sample answers, or answer-key sections. Mechanically enforced by
tools/lint_workbook.py (solution-leak markers, complete-implementation guard).
