## Context
The workbook scaffold originally shipped its own `python-crash-course/CLAUDE.md`.
Nested CLAUDE.md files load alongside the root one, producing two overlapping,
subtly divergent rule sets — exactly the drift this system exists to prevent.

## Decision
The repo-root CLAUDE.md is the ONLY operating contract. The workbook's style
rules were migrated into its "Workbook Style Rules" section and the nested
file was deleted (2026-07-01).

## Alternatives considered
- Keep both files, workbook one scoped to its folder — rejected: both load in
  a session at the repo root; deduplication by convention doesn't survive
  compaction (see DEC-0004).

## Consequences
All rule edits happen in one place. Enforced by RUL-0003 and a Drift
Self-Check bullet in CLAUDE.md; WORKFLOW.md documents the rationale.
