## Rule
A part may be marked beyond `written` in PROGRESS.md only after, in order:
(1) /tmp/verify_partN.py (reference solution per exercise + the EXACT asserts
copied from the part file) ran green under `pytest -q`; (2)
`python tools/lint_workbook.py part-N.md` passed; (3) PROGRESS.md was updated
and the /tmp file deleted.

## Rationale
Guarantees every shipped assert is achievable and every style rule held,
without checking solutions into the repo. Locked by DEC-0005.

## Exceptions
none
